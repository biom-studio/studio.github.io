ABOUT-US

BIOM, an exclusive architectural and interior design firm helmed by two visionary designers,
Ar. Chirag Manav and Ar. Shruti Dhanda, who are dedicated to crafting extraordinary spaces that captivate and inspire.
Renowned for our innovative and personalized design solutions, we have earned a reputation for delivering unparalleled excellence in residential and commercial projects
At BIOM, we firmly believe that collaboration is the essence of creating awe-inspiring spaces that truly embody our clients' unique needs and aspirations. We take immense pride in our ability to forge strong partnerships with our clients, guiding them through the design process and ensuring that every detail surpasses their expectations.
Our expertise spans architectural design, interior design, project management, and construction supervision, allowing us to provide a comprehensive range of services. We embrace the profound impact of design on lives and the environment, and thus, we are committed to creating spaces that are both functional and sustainable. With a strong focus on eco-friendly materials, energy-efficient technologies, and innovative design principles, we strive to make a positive difference in the world through our creations.
